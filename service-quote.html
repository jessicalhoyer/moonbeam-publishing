Service Quote

Take a look below to review our different service options and get your free quote!

Manuscript Revision

Have your manuscript critiqued and reviewed by our agents. Please choose how thoroughly you would like your manuscript to be reviewed. Prices will be dependent on the type of service and the number of pages of your manuscript you would like us to review.

Basic: General comments and general impression will be given.

Revision: Manuscript will be edited according to grammar and styling rules. Mild comments may be made.

Deluxe: Manuscript will be thoroughly read, revised, and comments will be made throughout.

Complete: Manuscript will be thoroughly read, revised, and comments will be made throughout. There will be one follow-up meeting afterward with an opportunity for further revisions.




Book Layout

Coordinating the layout of the final print of the book, including fonts, spacing, chapters, and any artwork or illustrations such as maps. Price is $200.

Cover Design

We employ several graphic designers who can craft the perfect cover for your novel.

Printing and Distribution

If your book is already edited and designed, we will be happy to print your book out for you! Bundle discounts are available. Price is $50 per copy. Minimum print is 5, maximum is 5,000. If you need more than 5,000 books printed, please feel free to contact us and we can discuss it further with you.

If you would like the additional service of distributing your book to booksellers (such as Barnes and Noble), we will be happy to ship your books out to them, hot off the press! Price is $50 per copy.
