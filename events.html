Upcoming Events

Sign up for our newsletter to keep up to date on all upcoming events!

Book Signing

Join one of our recently published authors for a book signing at our Minneapolis, Minnesota location! They will be signing their newest book from 11am to 3pm in the upstairs loft!

Writing Workshop

Join our group of teachers and volunteers who will be helping school and college-aged people write essays and creative works from 1pm to 4pm in the University of Minnesota library! If you would like to attend or if you would like to volunteer for the event, please register.

NaNoWriMo 2019

National Novel Writing Month begins in November 2019! Are you prepared? Please go ahead and download our advice guide and plan to attend one of our NaNo workshops! If you would like to volunteer for a workshop or if you would like to create a new workshop of your own in your neighborhood, please Contact Us!

Book Release Party

Every month we host an event to celebrate our newly published book and their newly published authors! Join us on the first Tuesday of every month to show these newbies in love! They'll be available to sign their books and answer your questions. Refreshments will be provided! If you would like to attend, please register!

Fundraising Event

On the first Friday of every month, we will be hosting fundraising events for the Moonbeam Foundation! All proceeds will go to helping students buy reading materials and textbooks required for their classes. If you would like to attend, please register!
