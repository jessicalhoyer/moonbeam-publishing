Our Services

Here at Moonbeam Publishing, we offer all of the services necessary to make your book a reality.

Manuscript Revision

We have a dedicated team of literary agents who will work hard to read, edit, and make comments on your manuscript. If you do not have a full manuscript ready, we can also critique individual chapters for a reduced fee.

Book Layout and Cover Design

We employ several graphic designers who can craft the perfect cover for your novel. They will also figure out the layout of the final print of the book, including fonts, spacing, chapters, and any artwork or illustrations such as maps.

Printing

If your book is already edited and designed, we will be happy to print your book out for you! Bundle discounts are available.

Distribution

If you would like the additional service of distributing your book to booksellers (such as Barnes and Noble), we will be happy to ship your books out to them, hot off the press!

Selling

We can also sell a copy of your book in our location in Minneapolis, Minnesota, as well as at our online store.
